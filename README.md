## Personal Statement

When I think about ethics, I see it as doing the right thing even when it’s not the easiest option. It’s easy to say you care about fairness or honesty, but what really matters is how those values show up in your actions. For me, ethics means being aware of how my decisions affect other people and taking responsibility for the outcomes.

This also applies to AI and technology. Even though AI can make things faster and more efficient, I think it’s important to think about who might be impacted by those systems. If something is biased or unfair, it shouldn’t just be ignored for the sake of convenience. The people creating and using these systems should take the time to question their decisions and consider the consequences.

Overall, I see ethics as something you practice every day. It’s about being thoughtful, accountable, and making choices that prioritize people, whether that’s in everyday life or when working with technology like AI.
